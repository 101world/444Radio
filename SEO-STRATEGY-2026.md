## ðŸŽ¨ On-Page SEO Best Practices

### Title Tag Formula
```
[Primary Keyword] | [USP] | [Brand] - [India-specific]
Example: "AI Music Generator | Free 20 Credits | 444Radio - India's First"
```

### Meta Description Formula
```
[Action verb] + [Primary keyword] + [Benefit] + [CTA] + [Free offer]
Example: "Create AI-generated music in seconds! India's #1 AI music generator. Generate songs, beats from text prompts. Free 20 credits with code: FREE THE MUSIC."
```

### H1-H6 Structure
- **H1:** India's First AI Music Generator (only one per page)
- **H2:** Key features, benefits, how-to sections
- **H3:** Sub-features, FAQs
- Use keywords naturally in headings

### Content Guidelines
- **Minimum 300 words** per page for indexing
- **1-2% keyword density** (natural, not forced)
- Include **synonyms and variations** of keywords
- Add **internal links** to related pages
- Use **bullet points and lists** for scannability
- Include **multimedia** (images, videos, audio players)
